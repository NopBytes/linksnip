LinkSnip Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

LinkSnip is a Chrome extension that lets you drag-select a region on any webpage and automatically open all hyperlinks within that region in new tabs.

Data Collection

LinkSnip does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. No analytics, no telemetry, no servers.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions solely to perform its core function:

  • activeTab - to interact with the current page when you activate the snip tool
  • scripting - to inject the selection overlay into the page
  • tabs - to open detected links in new background tabs

Third Parties

LinkSnip does not integrate with any third-party services and does not send data anywhere.
